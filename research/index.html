Industry Input-Output Modeling

Supply Chains for Sustainable Communities
Community IO applications using the US EPA's new environmentally-extended input-output API. The Model Earth website combines environmental impact charts with county industry levels using employment and payroll data for community-focused product Life Cycle Assessment (LCA).

Disaster Mobility Patterns

Impact of Extreme Storms on Human Mobility
Analyzing changes in mobility patterns during and following storms can inform recovery spending and the creation of tax incentives geared toward positive environmental outcomes when restoring civic services and planning new energy infrastructure.

Privacy on the Digital Commons

CrowdSense - Multicamera, Multikey Privacy
In the midst of a rapid expansion of spatial data gathering, we find ourselves caught in a digital version of the Tragedy of the Commons, in which siloed firms fail to exchange de-identified spatial data due to the lack of standards for privacy protection and peer-authorized data recovery.
