Privacy on the Digital Commons

CrowdSense - Multicamera, Multikey Privacy
In the midst of a rapid expansion in spatial data gathering technology, we find our data caught in a digital version of the Tragedy of the Commons, in which siloed firms fail to exchange de-identified spatial data due to a lack of standards for privacy protection and peer-authorized data recovery.
